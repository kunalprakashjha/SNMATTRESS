Our Story

A Gwalior workshop, a nationwide promise.

Founded in 2013 as S.N. Mattress Pvt. Ltd., we set out with a single conviction — that the quality of a person's day begins the night before. Over twelve years that conviction grew into a craft, the craft grew into two trusted brands, and the brands grew into a pan-India network of dealers and distributors.

Today, rebranded as Prime Lotus Mattress, we manufacture for homes, hotels, hospitals, hostels and institutions across more than two hundred Indian cities. Every mattress we send out is engineered for the realities of Indian living — long summers, joint families, varying body needs, and price points that work hard for every kind of household.

We deliver free across our service zones, install on the same visit, and stand behind every piece with a written warranty. The goal has not changed since day one: better mornings, made one mattress at a time.

Common Questions

Before you shop.

A handful of the questions we get asked most often. If yours isn't listed, our team is one call or WhatsApp message away.

  • Which mattress is best for the Indian climate?
    For most of India's hot, humid months, breathable options like rubberized coir, gel-infused foam, or hybrid (foam over springs) work best. They allow airflow and resist heat retention. For air-conditioned bedrooms or colder northern winters, memory foam and latex perform beautifully.
  • What warranty do you offer, and what does it cover?
    Warranty ranges from 5 to 10 years depending on the category — covering manufacturing defects, sagging beyond stated tolerance, and core material integrity. Full written terms are shared at time of purchase, and our service team handles every claim end-to-end.
  • Do you deliver across India? Is there a delivery charge?
    Yes — we ship to 200+ cities through our dealer and logistics network. Free delivery and installation are included across our primary service zones. For interior or remote PIN codes, a nominal logistics fee may apply, which our team will confirm before order confirmation.
  • I have lower-back pain. Soft, medium or firm?
    For chronic lower-back issues, a medium-firm orthopedic or hybrid mattress is generally most supportive — it keeps the spine aligned without flattening pressure points. Side sleepers usually want a touch softer; back and stomach sleepers benefit from firmer. Call us and we'll match you to the right one.
  • Do you offer custom sizes for cots and divans?
    Absolutely. Indian cot sizes vary widely — and we make to measure. Send us your bed's internal length × width × preferred height, and we'll quote a custom mattress in 2–3 working days. Most categories are available in custom sizing.
  • Do you handle bulk orders for hotels, hostels and hospitals?
    Yes — institutional supply is one of our core verticals. We work with hotel chains, hostels, hospitals, senior living facilities and PG operators. Volume pricing, GST invoicing, scheduled deliveries, and on-site installation are all standard. Write to us with your requirement and timeline.
  • How should I care for my mattress to make it last?
    Use a fitted mattress protector from day one — it protects against dust mites, sweat and accidental spills. Rotate the mattress 180° every 3 months. Vacuum gently every 4–6 weeks. Avoid jumping on it (we know, we know). Sun-air it once a quarter where possible.
